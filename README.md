# Privacy Policy for Cue

Last updated: 07 November 2025

## Data Collection
Cue does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information is stored locally on your device.

## Data Storage
- Smoking history and statistics are stored in your device's local storage
- Dark mode preferences are stored locally
- In-app purchase status is stored locally

## Third-Party Services
- AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device and usage information. See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Play Billing: Used for in-app purchases. See Google's privacy policy.

## Data Deletion
You can delete all app data by:
1. Using the "Reset Progress" feature in the app
2. Uninstalling the app
